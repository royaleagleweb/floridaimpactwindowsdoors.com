About Impact Doors

Hurricane-Rated Doors Built for the South Florida Lifestyle

Doors represent some of the largest and most vulnerable openings in any South Florida home. When a hurricane strikes, unprotected doors can blow in under extreme wind pressure, allowing rain, wind, and debris to devastate your interior. Impact doors are specifically engineered to resist these forces, combining reinforced frames with laminated impact glass that holds together even when struck by wind-borne debris traveling at over 100 miles per hour.

At Florida Impact Windows & Doors, we install the complete range of impact door styles demanded by South Florida homeowners. Our most popular product is the impact sliding glass door, which is essential for the lanai, patio, and pool-deck living that defines life in Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and the Palm Beaches. We also install impact French doors for formal living and dining areas, impact entry doors that make a stunning first impression, and impact patio doors designed for seamless indoor-outdoor transitions.

Every impact door installation begins with precise measurements taken at your home by our experienced project managers. Because doors bear significant structural and wind loads, proper sizing and installation are critical. Our certified installers use manufacturer-specified fasteners, anchoring systems, and waterproofing techniques to ensure every door performs as designed during a hurricane. We then coordinate all necessary permits and inspections with your local building department so you receive full code compliance documentation.

The aesthetic options available in today's impact doors are remarkable. Modern manufacturing techniques allow for ultra-slim frame profiles that maximize glass area and views, premium powder-coat finishes in dozens of colors, decorative glass options for entry doors, and hardware packages ranging from minimalist contemporary to classic coastal. Whether your South Florida home is a modern waterfront estate in Miami Beach, a Mediterranean-style villa in Coral Gables, or a coastal cottage in Delray Beach, there is an impact door design that will elevate your home's beauty while delivering full hurricane protection.

Beyond storm protection, impact doors deliver substantial daily benefits. The laminated glass significantly reduces outside noise, a real advantage in busy South Florida neighborhoods near airports, highways, and commercial districts. The Low-E coatings reflect solar heat, keeping your home cooler and reducing the workload on your air conditioning system. And the impact-rated glass plus multi-point locking hardware provide a formidable barrier against break-ins, giving your family added peace of mind every night.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Impact Doors

What types of impact doors are available for South Florida homes?

We install a full range of impact-rated doors including sliding glass doors, French doors, front entry doors, patio doors, pivot doors, and garage impact doors. Each type is available in multiple styles, colors, and finishes to complement your home's architecture. Sliding glass impact doors are the most popular choice in South Florida due to the region's indoor-outdoor lifestyle and waterfront living.

How much do impact doors cost in Miami and South Florida?

Impact door pricing depends on the type, size, glass options, and hardware selected. Standard impact sliding glass doors typically range from $1,500 to $4,000 installed, while premium entry doors with decorative glass can range from $2,000 to $6,000. French impact doors generally cost between $2,500 and $5,500. We provide free in-home estimates with detailed pricing and offer flexible financing options.

Can I replace my regular sliding glass door with an impact sliding door?

Yes. Impact sliding glass door replacement is one of the most common upgrades we perform in South Florida. In most cases, a new impact sliding door can be installed into the existing opening with minimal modifications to the surrounding wall or frame. Our team handles the removal, installation, waterproofing, and finishing in a single visit, typically completing the work in half a day per door.

Do impact doors qualify for insurance discounts in Florida?

Absolutely. Impact-rated doors are a key component of a whole-home hurricane mitigation system. When all openings including windows, doors, and the garage door are protected with impact-rated products, South Florida homeowners qualify for the maximum wind mitigation discount on their insurance premiums, which can save 20% to 45% or more annually.

What is the difference between impact glass doors and standard doors with shutters?

Impact glass doors are engineered with laminated glass and reinforced frames that provide permanent hurricane protection without any preparation. Standard doors with shutters require manual deployment before each storm, can be difficult to operate, and provide no daily benefits. Impact doors additionally offer year-round noise reduction, UV protection, energy savings, and security.
