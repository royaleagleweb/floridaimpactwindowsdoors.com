Signs It's Time to Replace Your Doors in Plantation

Not sure if your doors need replacing? Watch for these common warning signs that Broward County homeowners encounter.

Visible Damage

Cracks, warping, rotting, or corrosion on the door frame, panels, or threshold that compromise structural integrity.

Drafts & Air Leaks

You can feel air moving around the door edges, or see daylight through gaps — a sign that seals and weatherstripping have failed.

Difficult Operation

Doors that stick, drag, won't latch properly, or jump off their tracks are past the point of simple repair.

High Energy Bills

Old single-pane or poorly insulated doors allow heat transfer that forces your AC to work harder in South Florida's climate.
