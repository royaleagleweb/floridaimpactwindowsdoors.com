What types of impact doors are best for Surfside homes?
The best impact door type for your Surfside home depends on the opening location and your lifestyle. Sliding glass impact doors are ideal for patio and pool areas, French impact doors suit formal living spaces, and reinforced entry doors provide maximum front-door security. Impact doors installed in Surfside require minimal maintenance.
